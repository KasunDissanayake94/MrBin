MrBin a Smart Garbage Management System developed in order to make an eco-friendly environment by using effective garbage collecting mechanism.The Garbage management system is initiated covering all the public institutes in Colombo aid in minimising the Environmental pollution that occured before.



This system will facilitates the functionalities to the Urban Council to colect filled garbage bins once it get the notifications on those bins.This system has aimed at highly populated organizations.Based on the optimized routes generated by the system,Urban Council will direct the Garbage collectors to collect garbage
